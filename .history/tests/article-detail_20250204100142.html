How to Maintain a Healthy Heart

By Dr. Anna Nguyen | Published on Jan 15, 2025

Maintaining a healthy heart is crucial for overall well-being. Regular exercise, a balanced diet, and stress management play key roles in cardiovascular health. Avoiding smoking and limiting alcohol consumption are also important preventive measures.

Key Tips for a Healthy Heart:

  • Eat a diet rich in vegetables, fruits, and whole grains.
  • Exercise for at least 30 minutes a day.
  • Manage stress through meditation or relaxation techniques.
  • Get regular check-ups and monitor blood pressure levels.
